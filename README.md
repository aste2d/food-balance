## Overview

Spice up your survival experience with **Food Balance** – a *vanilla-friendly datapack* that redefines how food works, perfect for players who want **fresh mechanics** without drifting from the core Minecraft feel.

🔗 **Synergy:** Compatible with **[Apple Enchant](https://modrinth.com/datapack/apple-enchant)** and **[Mooshroom Energy](https://modrinth.com/datapack/energy)** for expanded food mechanics!

---

## Features

### Vanilla-Friendly
- Seamless integration into your world.
- No major gameplay changes.
- Only needs a **server-side install** for multiplayer!

### Unique Food Effects
- Every food has a purpose.
- No two items share the same effect.
- All foods are balanced — **no absolute best food**.

### Buffed Forgotten Foods
- Useless items are no more!
- Formerly ignored foods now have **meaningful, fun mechanics**.

## Examples

- **Farming meals** grant **extra hearts** for half a Minecraft day.
- **Spider eyes** offer **Night Vision** and **Weaving**.
- **Rotten flesh** turns you **half-undead** — spooky!
- **Honey bottles** become your best friend in the mines.
- And finally... **Rabbit Stew** is worth crafting (and it’s HUGE)!
